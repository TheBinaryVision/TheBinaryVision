### About Me
Welcome to my digital canvas, where binary code meets visionary creations. I am a Computer Science graduate and a Full-Stack Developer specializing in the **MERN stack**, passionate about transforming complex logic into elegant, user-centric applications.

Beyond writing clean code, my background includes leadership roles as a **Google Developer Group (GDG) Coordinator** and **Training & Placement Cell Secretary**, which taught me the value of community, mentorship, and collaboration in tech.

When I am not debugging or exploring new AI capabilities, you can find me behind a camera lens capturing stories, designing digital art, or on the field playing cricket.
